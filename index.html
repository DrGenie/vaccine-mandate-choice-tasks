To mandate or not to mandate? Vaccine Mandate Survey (For Research Team Only!)

Understanding Vaccine Mandate Features

Welcome! In this survey, we’re looking at how vaccination policies might be designed if a new pandemic (different from COVID‑19) were to emerge, and safe, effective vaccines were available.

You’ll first see tutorials on six key features of a potential vaccine mandate: scope, threshold, coverage, incentives, exemptions, and cost. Hover or tap on the small icons (e.g., ⚠️, 🌐, 💰) for more detailed explanations of each level.

Afterwards, you’ll pick the block you’ve been assigned (1–4). Each block has 9 scenarios. In each scenario, you’ll compare Vaccine Mandate A to Vaccine Mandate B and decide which you prefer, then whether you’d opt out if possible. We’ll attempt to email your final answers; if that fails, you can download them as CSV. Thank you for taking part!
