Understanding Vaccine Mandate Characteristics

In this section, we would like to understand how the characteristics of a vaccine mandate influence your choice. Please review the following slides describing six key characteristics. When you are ready, you will complete nine choice tasks.

Scope of Mandate

High‑risk occupations only: ⚠️ Vaccination is required only for individuals in critical roles.

All occupations and public spaces: 🌐 Vaccination is required for everyone.

Infection Threshold for Mandate Activation

50 cases per 100k with 10% weekly increase: 🟢

100 cases per 100k with 15% weekly increase: 🟠

200 cases per 100k with 20% weekly increase: 🔴

Vaccine Coverage Requirement for Lifting Mandates

Low (50%): Mandates are lifted when 50% are vaccinated.

Moderate (70%): A balanced threshold.

High (90%): Requires near‑universal vaccination.

Incentives for Vaccination

No incentives: 🚫

Paid time off (1–3 days): 🕒

Government subsidies/discounts: 💸

Exemption Policy

Medical exemptions only: 🩺

Medical and religious exemptions: 🩺🙏

Medical, religious and broad personal belief exemptions: 🩺🙏💡

Opportunity Cost of Compliance

Low (A$5): $

Moderate (A$20): $ $

High (A$50): $ $ $
